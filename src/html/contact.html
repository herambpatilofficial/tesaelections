Why us?

What sets us apart is our shared passion for innovation and our deep-rooted commitment to the betterment of TESA. We understand the unique challenges and opportunities that students pursuing telecommunication engineering face, and we are eager to address these in creative ways.

Our vision is to transform TESA into a dynamic hub where members can thrive academically, socially, and professionally. By choosing us, you're not just electing individuals but a dedicated team ready to work collaboratively and tirelessly on your behalf. Together, we will propel TESA to new heights, making it a place where every telecommunication engineering student can excel and find the support they need to succeed.

Strengths

Experience: A proven track record in student leadership.
Diversity: Diverse backgrounds and ideas for creative problem-solving.
Commitment: Deep dedication to TESA's betterment, not just during elections.
Innovation: Ready to introduce fresh, industry-relevant initiatives.
Collaboration: A united, seamless team ready to work together.
Accessibility: Always available and approachable for all TESA members.
Long-term Vision: Planning for both short-term success and a strong future.

Room for Betterment

We are firm believers in open communication and constant improvement. Your ideas, expectations, and concerns are invaluable to us. We want to hear from you directly. Please feel free to reach out to us with your suggestions, thoughts, or any ideas you have for enhancing TESA. Your input will play a crucial role in shaping the future of our association. Together, we can make TESA.
